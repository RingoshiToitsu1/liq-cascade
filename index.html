Cascade below the high

Simple isolated-margin formula used: long liquidation ≈ entry × (1 − 1/leverage + maintenance%), short liquidation ≈ entry × (1 + 1/leverage − maintenance%). A small fee/funding buffer is subtracted from the cushion to account for trading fees and funding drift, which nudge real liquidation prices closer to entry than the textbook formula suggests. This ignores cross-margin, partial liquidation tiers, and exchange-specific mark-price methods — real liquidation prices vary by venue.
No data is stored or sent anywhere except the public price feed. Recompute anytime — nothing here is financial advice.
